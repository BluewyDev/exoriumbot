Privacy policy
Here we cover the privacy policies for both the website and the bot.

Website Privacy Policy

This website was objectively made to display information, features, commands and facts about the exorium bot. We should not need to store any user data or cookies for this and we will not. The "log in" button does not have a real functionality, and does not log you in. This button only functions as a small side-joke and does NOT serve any other purpose.

Bot Privacy Policy

exorium does not store any information at the moment. The only further relative information is that we log guild joins/leaves by the bot, as well as commands that were executed. If you have any questions however, you are always free to contact one of our developers.
