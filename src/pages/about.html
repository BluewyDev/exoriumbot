exorium goal

exorium (lowercase "e") is a bot developed with the mission to provide users and guilds with a new, better interaction command system, also allowing for preference options and more. Not only that, exorium strives to become the next new bot to offer users the best of help with their use of discord by offering utility commands that can help in any needed way.

What we offer:
We offer the following features, and always try our best to add more as well as to improve existent features.
Interaction

exorium is specialized in bringing the best of interaction commands forward to communities. We wish to give users an even better time using the many interaction commands we offer, and will proceed to make.

Utility

exorium is designed to offer a user-friendly experience with the bot, but also with discord. Our utility commands are designed to help you out in easing the use of discord, while also being handy and informative.

Moderation

exorium also offers moderation commands. Use them to enhance your server with better moderation commands, and to get a safer environment for all the members in your community.
